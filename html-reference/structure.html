Mrs. Chicken Weekly Report

A self-contained article about Mrs. Chicken’s daily routine, favorite tools, and observations from the coop.

Daily Habits

Mrs. Chicken maintains a strict morning routine and an even stricter policy on premium feed quality.

Morning Routine

  • Wake up at sunrise
  • Inspect the coop
    • Check nesting boxes
      • Count eggs carefully
      • Rearrange straw if needed
    • Inspect the water dish
  • Demand breakfast immediately

Preferred Task Order

  1. Cluck at the gardener
  2. Patrol the fence line
  3. Review feed supplies
  4. Supervise egg production
  5. Take a nap in the sun

Important Equipment

Coop

Perch
A raised bar used for resting above ground level.
